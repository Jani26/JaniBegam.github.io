Bank Churn Analysis

This project analyzes customer churn data for SD Bank using Tableau. The goal is to identify key factors leading to customer attrition and provide actionable insights to improve retention strategies.

Technologies Used

  • Tableau
  • Data Visualization
  • Customer Analytics

Key Steps

  1. Data Exploration and Preparation
  2. Identifying High-Risk Customers
  3. Analyzing Customer Demographics
  4. Visualization of Churn Trends

Key Findings

- High-income customers churn at a lower rate compared to low-income groups.
- Customers with fewer customer service interactions are more likely to churn.
- Specific card types and age groups exhibit higher churn rates.
