Key Performance Questions

  • How well do actual sales align with budgeted projections?
  • Which product categories are the most profitable?
  • Which provinces drive the highest sales volume?
  • How do costs compare to budgeted values?

Executive Dashboard Insights

The executive dashboard highlights top-performing products, key sales trends across provinces, and cost efficiencies. Notable insights include:

  • British Columbia leads in both coffee and food sales.
  • Major and small markets drive the highest sales volume.
  • Colombian Coffee is the most profitable product.
